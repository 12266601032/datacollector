Metric Alerts

Metric alerts are alerts based on pipeline and stage statistics, such as a high rate of bad records or a batch of records taking too long to process.

Metric alerts display in the Data Collector window in Monitoring mode. You can also configure a metric alert to send email to all email addresses associated with the pipeline.

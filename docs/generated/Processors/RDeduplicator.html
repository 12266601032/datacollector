Record Deduplicator

The Record Deduplicator discards records from the pipeline that include duplicate data. The Record Deduplicator routes data to two streams, one for unique records and one for duplicate records. Use the Record Deduplicator to route or discard duplicate data.

The Record Deduplicator can compare entire records or a subset of fields. Use a subset of fields to focus the comparison of duplicate data on the fields of particular concern. For example, to discard duplicate purchase events, you might compare information about the purchaser, selected items, and shipping address, but ignore the timestamp of the event.

To enhance pipeline performance, the Deduplicator hashes the comparison fields and uses the hashed values when evaluating for duplicates.

  • Comparison Window

    The Record Deduplicator compares records until it reaches the record limit. Then, it discards the information kept in memory and starts fresh. You can configure a time limit to trigger a refresh at regular time intervals as well.
